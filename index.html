Who I Am

Akashpreet Singh

I became a developer to create applications that improve the quality of life of its users, not make them more difficult. This desire grew during my time as a Data Analyst in various industries and during graduate school where I completed my M.B.A. focusing on Financial Securities and Investments. As a developer, I want to apply these prior experiences.

What I Do

Right below are some projects I have worked on as a developer; in some cases, being the solo developer, and in others a particular role.
